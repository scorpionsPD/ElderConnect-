**Analysis Date**: February 23, 2024

## 📊 Good Life Sorted - What They Do Well

**What Good Life Sorted Does Right:**
- ✅ 12+ service categories (companionship, home help, care, cleaning, etc.)
- ✅ Enhanced DBS checks & fully insured helpers
- ✅ References verification & training requirements
- ✅ Multi-region coverage (9+ UK regions)
- ✅ Local helper matching algorithm
- ✅ Rating & review system
- ✅ Social proof (98% recommend)
- ✅ Simple 4-step onboarding process
- ✅ Council partnerships

**Their Limitations:**
- ❌ Web-only (no mobile app)
- ❌ No real-time messaging (phone/email only)
- ❌ No video calling capability
- ❌ No emergency SOS feature
- ❌ No health tracking
- ❌ England-only (no Scotland-first option)
- ❌ Charges £20+/hour (people must pay)
- ❌ Commission-based model (20-25%)
